## *Option Volatility and Pricing*

<img src="../images/options-volatility.jpg" alt="Option Volatility and Pricing" width="200"/>

- **Author**: Sheldon Natenberg
- **Publisher**: McGraw-Hill
- **Year**: 1994
- **Difficulty**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Recommendation**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Overview

A classic in the field of options trading, this book provides an authoritative treatment of option pricing theory and volatility-based trading strategies. It covers options fundamentals, volatility analysis, delta-neutral strategies, options portfolio management, and risk hedging techniques, equipping readers with a comprehensive understanding of the core concepts and advanced applications in the options market.

### Key Chapters

1. Options Theory Fundamentals
2. Volatility Analysis
3. Delta-Neutral Strategies
4. Options Portfolio Management
5. Risk Hedging Techniques
