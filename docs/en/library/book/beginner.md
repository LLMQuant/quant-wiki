## *Quantitative Investment Strategies and Techniques*

<img src="../images/quant-strategy.jpg" alt="Quantitative Investment Strategies and Techniques" width="200"/>

- **Author**: Ding Peng
- **Publisher**: Publishing House of Electronics Industry
- **Year**: 2012
- **Difficulty**: ⭐⭐⭐
- **Recommendation**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Overview

This book provides a systematic introduction to the fundamental concepts of quantitative investing, strategy development methodologies, and risk management techniques. Through more than 60 case studies, it explores a wide range of quantitative investment strategies, including quantitative stock selection, market timing, index futures arbitrage, commodity futures arbitrage, statistical arbitrage, options arbitrage, algorithmic trading, and asset allocation. On the technical side, the book covers advanced mathematical tools such as artificial intelligence, data mining, wavelet analysis, support vector machines, fractal theory, and stochastic processes, with detailed explanations of how these techniques are applied in finance. It is ideal for fund managers, product managers, securities analysts, investment directors, and anyone aspiring to work in financial investment -- particularly readers with some financial background who wish to deepen their understanding of quantitative strategies and techniques.

### Key Chapters

1. Overview of Quantitative Investing
2. Equity Quantitative Strategies
3. Futures Quantitative Strategies
4. Statistical Arbitrage
5. Risk Management
