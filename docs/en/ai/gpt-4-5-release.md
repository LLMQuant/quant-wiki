# Responding to DeepSeek? OpenAI Releases GPT-4.5, Billed as Its "Most Powerful" Model

**Now available to Pro users and developers worldwide**

> **"GPT-4.5 represents a new leap in pre-training and post-training scale. It can identify patterns, draw connections, and generate more creative insights through unsupervised learning -- though it does not possess explicit reasoning capabilities."**

Early testing indicates that conversations with GPT-4.5 feel more natural. It has a broader knowledge base, better understands user intent, and demonstrates higher "emotional intelligence." GPT-4.5 excels in applications such as writing improvement, coding, and real-world problem solving, while also **reducing hallucinations**.

OpenAI has released GPT-4.5 as a research preview to better understand the model's strengths and limitations. What can GPT-4.5 actually achieve? OpenAI looks forward to the community discovering its potential and boundaries through real-world usage.

---

## Scaling Unsupervised Learning

> **"OpenAI advances AI capabilities by simultaneously scaling two paradigms: unsupervised learning and reasoning."**

- **Reasoning**: Enables the model to think and reason before answering, particularly suited for complex STEM or logic problems. Models like OpenAI o1 and OpenAI o3-mini represent this approach.
- **Unsupervised learning**: Through exposure to vast quantities of unlabeled data, the model accumulates richer world knowledge and sharper intuition.

GPT-4.5 is the product of **scaled unsupervised learning**. It was trained on Microsoft's Azure AI supercomputers, with innovations in model architecture and optimization algorithms. The combination of massive data and compute gives the model broader and deeper world understanding, **reducing hallucinations** and providing more reliable information across a wide range of topics.

## Extending the GPT Paradigm

On the GPT series timeline, GPT-4.5 (2025) marks a new milestone.

> **Sample question: What was the first language?**

GPT-4.5's response highlights multiple perspectives on human language origins and emphasizes that the "first language" likely emerged in oral form long ago, leaving little surviving evidence. It draws on historical, cultural, and academic viewpoints, demonstrating a deep mastery of world knowledge.

## Training for Human Collaboration

> **"As models grow in scale and tackle increasingly complex problems, they must also develop a deeper understanding of human needs and intent."**

During GPT-4.5's training, OpenAI employed novel scalable techniques that allow large models to learn from smaller models in a secondary training phase. This makes GPT-4.5 more "steerable" -- better at inferring user intent and maintaining a natural, human-like conversational tone.

## Use Cases

### 1. Helping Users Through Difficult Moments

**GPT-4.5:**
> "It sounds like this exam result really hit you hard. I understand -- that kind of setback can be demoralizing, but it doesn't define you or your potential. Would you like to talk about what happened? Or would a different kind of distraction help? I'm here either way."

**GPT-4o:**
> Provides 6 practical suggestions such as "analyze what went wrong," "talk to your professor or teacher," and "actively seek support" -- useful but with a more detached tone.

GPT-4.5's response prioritizes emotional connection, naturally inviting the user to share their feelings, demonstrating **higher emotional intelligence (EQ)**.

### 2. Identifying Paintings

**GPT-4.5:**
> "You're likely referring to Claude Lorrain's *The Trojan Women Setting Fire to Their Fleet*. Painted in 1643, it depicts the Trojan women burning their ships after their exhausting journey, inspired by Virgil's *Aeneid*."

**GPT-4o:**
> Identified the work as *The Women of Cnidus Burning the Fleet*, with some inaccuracy in attribution.

GPT-4.5 not only identifies the painting more accurately but also provides richer historical context.

### 3. Discussing Space Exploration

**GPT-4.5:**
> "OpenAI believes space exploration is not a luxury but an essential pathway for Earth to address climate change, resource scarcity, and existential risks. It also drives technological innovation and economic growth, inspiring humanity to work toward shared goals."

**GPT-4o:**
> Provides a longer discussion of the importance and ethical dimensions of space exploration, but tends more toward information listing.

GPT-4.5's response is more concise and conversational in its emotional and intentional expression.

## Stronger Reasoning Is on the Way

It is worth emphasizing that GPT-4.5 **does not "think before answering"** -- its strengths derive primarily from the broad knowledge and more precise expression that unsupervised learning provides. By contrast, reasoning-focused models like OpenAI o1 and o3-mini are better suited for complex STEM problems.

OpenAI believes that as pre-trained models like GPT-4.5 continue expanding along the "knowledge" dimension, this accumulated "world understanding" will form a robust foundation for future integration of reasoning and tool-use capabilities.

---

## Safety

> **"Every improvement in model capability creates an opportunity to further optimize safety."**

GPT-4.5's training incorporated new supervision techniques alongside traditional **supervised fine-tuning (SFT)** and **reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF)**. Safety testing under OpenAI's **Preparedness Framework** indicates that scaling the GPT paradigm improves capabilities across all assessment dimensions. OpenAI will publish complete evaluation results in the accompanying system card, laying groundwork for future, more advanced alignment work.

## Using GPT-4.5 in ChatGPT

Starting immediately, **ChatGPT Pro users** can switch to GPT-4.5 in the model selector on web, mobile, and desktop. OpenAI will extend access to Plus and Team users the following week, and to Enterprise and Edu users the week after that.

> - GPT-4.5 can access up-to-date search information
> - Supports file and image uploads
> - Works with Canvas for writing and coding
> - Does not yet support multimodal features in ChatGPT (voice mode, video, screen sharing)

In the future, OpenAI will continue simplifying the user experience so that AI "automatically adapts" to different use cases.

---

## Using GPT-4.5 via the API

OpenAI has also made GPT-4.5 available in preview to all paid users (including Chat Completions API, Assistants API, and Batch API). The model supports function calling, Structured Outputs, streaming, and system messages, and can accept image inputs for vision capabilities.

Given GPT-4.5's strengths in emotional understanding and creativity, it is **well-suited for writing assistance, communication, learning companionship, coaching, and brainstorming**. It also demonstrates strong execution in multi-step programming and complex automation tasks.

> **"GPT-4.5 is large and computationally expensive, and may not directly replace GPT-4o. OpenAI will decide whether to maintain GPT-4.5 in the API long-term based on developer feedback."**
